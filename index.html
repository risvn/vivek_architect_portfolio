About Me

Hi, I’m Talla Vivek, a passionate Civil Engineer and Freelance Designer based in Hyderabad. I have worked on over 10 projects in and around the city, collaborating closely with SPACEUIX to bring innovative architectural and structural designs to life.

With a keen eye for detail and a user-focused design approach, I specialize in transforming floor plans and elevations into functional, aesthetic spaces that elevate the end-user experience. Whether it's residential, commercial, or custom design work, I aim to deliver quality and creativity in every project.

Let’s build something exceptional together.
